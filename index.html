School Shooting Analysis

Welcome to the Landing Page!

Summary

School shootings have become an unfortunate trend in the US over the past several years. With this project we are attempting to visualize those trends and determine if there have been any increases or decreases in shootings over a 30-year period, starting from 1990 until now. *Follow the links below to see a table breakdown by year and state of the incident database and detailed and specific graphs on frequency of shootings.

Incident Database

This page will allow you to filter a state and year of your choosing, and you will be able to see all the incidents and the details for that given state and year


Frequencies

This page shows data visualizations of the data that shows the number of shootings per year. It will allow you to filter these visualizations by School type, area type or total accross the USA. This will also allow you to see the total shootings per state
